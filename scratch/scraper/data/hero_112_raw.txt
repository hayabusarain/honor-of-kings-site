Background
[
]
In the eyes of the people, Butterfly is a top-notch mercenary with neither emotion nor desire. Rumor has it that the reason she works so hard to complete her mission is to repay a debt to the Mercenary Guild. However, what no one knows is that this girl, orphaned since childhood, has certain fond memories of her childhood despite her arduous training under the guild, thanks to a man named
Allain
. Thus, she has followed his trail to
Navenia
, vowing to find her precious companion even if he no longer remembers her.
Lore
[
]
Orphaned from birth, and raised by the Mercenary Guild, Butterfly was sent to the School of Steel after attracting attention with her sword fighting talent. There, she met Allain, and the two orphans quickly became friends, supporting and taking care of each other. Their time at the school became their most precious shared memory.
Having passed the evaluation at the school and returning to the guild, she now serves the guild per the regulations. The guild takes half of her earnings to repay the money they have invested in her, plus a little interest. She will be able to terminate her contract with them only after the debt is fully paid, at which point she can become a freelance mercenary, or pursue whatever other endeavor that might take her fancy.
Since returning, she has completed plenty of A-level jobs, and one S-level job without fail, making her one of the best mercenaries in the guild.
Recently, while investigating a mysterious incident, she ran into Allain, her old friend from the School of Steel. However, he seems to have lost all of his memories.
As she investigates the incident further, she's also looking for a way to help him recall his past...
