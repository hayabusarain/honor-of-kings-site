Flawless skin  is the second higher and expensive skin in game (the first one is Mythic). Flawless Type Skin has 7 skins (Global + China).
Although Flawless have many feature more than Mythic skins but Flawless skin cost cheaper than Mythic.
Ways To Get
[
]
Global: Flawless skins can get by Gacha and it cost 4000-6000 tokens
China: You can exchange by 2 Gacha Prize Tickets, and Prize Tickets can get from Gacha or friend's gift
