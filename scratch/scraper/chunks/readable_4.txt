Introduction [ edit ] Luna is a free-spirited warrior blessed by the moon.  Proud and resolute, she presents herself with a commanding aura. However, she has experienced her share of darkness. When she was just a young girl, Kaizer , her older brother whom she adored with all her heart, went on a rampage and slaughtered everyone in their family. He had always been kind to her, such that she could not believe he was capable of such a wicked act. Indeed, it was the curse upon their ancient clan that had driven him to act against it: Only one member of the clan could inherit its wondrous powers. She had been chosen, and he had not. Luna understood her brother's suffering and would not fight him. When she willingly offered her life to her brother's sword, he found it impossible to bring it down upon her. Now, Luna spends her time searching for her lost brother, becoming ever stronger during her journey.
