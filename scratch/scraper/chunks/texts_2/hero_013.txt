He also participated in the building of Chang'an, and he personally guards the city with his machines. Relying on his extraordinary talent for piloting them, Mozi and his machinery have become symbols of peace. Skills [ edit ] Passive Skill: Fight for Peace [ edit ] Fight for Peace Passive Skill Ability Type Crowd Control Shield Damage Magic Every 4th Basic Attack deals area damage and knocks back enemies . Gains shield stacks when using skills, for up to 2 stacks. Skill 1: Peacebringer [ edit ] Peacebringer Skill 1 Ability Type Movement Enhance Damage Magic Dashes in the target direction, dealing damage to enemies in his path. The next Basic Attack is enhanced , becoming Fight for Peace. Skill 2: Cannon Blast [ edit ] Cannon Blast Skill 2 Ability Type Crowd Control Damage Magic Fires a shell in the target direction, stunning and dealing area damage to the 1st enemy hit. The shell leaves a crater in the ground after reaching its max range of hitting an enemy dealing damage over time to enemies that step in it. Skill 3: Think Inside the Box [ edit ] Think Inside the Box Skill 3 Ability Type Crowd Control Damage Magic Deals damage over time to enemies within a certain range of himself, stunning enemies every time he deals damage.
