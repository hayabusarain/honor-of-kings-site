Contents 1 Introduction 2 Skills 2.1 Passive Skill: Hourglass of Time 2.2 Skill 1: Time Bomb 2.3 Skill 2: Time Flux 2.4 Skill 3: Time Lapse 3 Splash Arts 4 See Also Introduction [ edit ] Sun Bin has always been immensely gifted, but a bit on the quiet side. During his time at Jixia Academy, even though he could manipulate the forces of machinery and even time itself, his peers mostly knew him as a shy and unassuming boy. The only one who truly recognized his talent was one of his seniors, who grew envious and proceeded to lay a deadly trap for him. Though Sun Bin survived, he lost his legs and his best friend. Using his skills in the art of machinery, he created mechanical wings that made up for the loss of his legs, and the young genius now devotes himself to studying the mysteries of mechanics and time in hopes of one day regaining his lost friend. Skills [ edit ] Passive Skill: Hourglass of Time [ edit ] Hourglass of Time Passive Skill Ability Type Speed Up Gains a Movement Speed bonus after using skills. Skill 1: Time Bomb [ edit ] Time Bomb Skill 1 Ability Type Slow Damage Magic Fires a bomb in the target direction, which deals damage and attaches itself to the hit enemy. After a brief delay, inflicts damage and slow . Skill 2: Time Flux [ edit ] Time Flux Skill 2 Ability Type Speed Up Recovery Enhance Increases his and nearby teammates' Movement Speed . When this effect ends, he restores Health equal to 25% of the damage taken during the effect's duration. Skill 3: Time Lapse [ edit ] Time Lapse Skill 3 Ability Type Crowd Control Slow Damage Magic Throws a bomb in the target direction, inflicting area damage , slow , and silence upon coming contact with an enemy or reaching its max range. After a brief delay, the bomb deals area damage again.
