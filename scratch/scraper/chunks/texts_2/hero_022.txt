Contents 1 Introduction 2 Skills 2.1 Passive Skill: Reaper's Revenge 2.2 Skill 1: Blood Echo 2.3 Skill 2: Deathscythe 2.4 Skill 3: Lay Waste 3 Splash Arts Introduction [ edit ] When the Blood Clan invaded, a boy named Bai Qi was injured while protecting the young sovereign of Yong. Though he was healed by a sage, he was warned not to touch the wound. But as Bai Qi grew older, he ignored the warning and fought on to protect Yong, ending up heavily injured once more. Finally, he put on a mask that would allow the wound to fester, and the power of the Blood Clan to thoroughly fuse with his body. He never took it off again. From that point on, he would be known only as the God of War—a hunched, decaying monster that would sweep across the battlefield with his scythe. Skills [ edit ] Passive Skill: Reaper's Revenge [ edit ] Reaper's Revenge Passive Skill Ability Type Enhance Speed Up Gains Energy when dealing or taking damage, increasing his Movement Speed and enhancing his recovery effects. Skill 1: Blood Echo [ edit ] Blood Echo Skill 1 Ability Type Recovery Damage Physical Deals damage to nearby enemies and recovers Health. Charging up will boost the skill's effects. Passive : When taking damage, there is a chance to automatically trigger Skill 1. Skill 2: Deathscythe [ edit ] Deathscythe Skill 2 Ability Type Crowd Control Enhance Damage Magic Summons scythes in the target direction, pulling back enemies within range while inflicting damage and slow . Skill 3: Lay Waste [ edit ] Lay Waste Skill 3 Ability Type Crowd Control Enhance Damage Magic Leaps to the target location and taunts enemies while dealing damage and enhancing his recovery effects. Taunt duration increases with Max Health.
