To prove his skills, Liu Shan has been causing trouble from an early age. When war came to the streets of Yi, he abandoned his plans to conquer Jixia and joined a war the likes of which had never been seen. Liu Shan piloted his Panda Mecha One onto the battlefield, and the steampunk savant's unstoppable destructive power was impossible to ignore. In his case, the student has truly outdone the master.
