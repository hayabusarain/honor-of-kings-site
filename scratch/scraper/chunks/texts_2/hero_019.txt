Savage and determined, she stuck to her way of doing things even at Jixia Academy. From stealing tomes to working as a mercenary, no one could predict what her next wild step would be. Only when she shattered the chains that bound the feralis with her hammer did it finally dawn on people that this formidable warrior had become an unstoppable force. Skills [ edit ] Passive Skill: Petrifying Strike [ edit ] Petrifying Strike Passive Skill Ability Type Crowd Control Skill and Basic Attacks have a chance to petrify enemy heroes. Passive : Periodically gains a sheild . Skill 1: Storm and Stress [ edit ] Storm and Stress Skill 1 Ability Type Movement Enhance Damage Physical Dashes to the target direction and enhance the next Basic Attack to inflict extra damage and slow . Skill 2: Shock and Awe [ edit ] Shock and Awe Skill 2 Damage Physical Deals damage to enemies in range. Skill 3: Hurricane Hammer [ edit ] Hurricane Hammer Skill 3 Ability Type Speed Up Sheild Damage Physical Spins her hammer for a period of time, continuously dealing damage to nearby enemies. While the skill is active, gains a Movement Speed boost and sheild .
