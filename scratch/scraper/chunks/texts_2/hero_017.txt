Contents 1 Introduction 2 Skills 2.1 Passive Skill: Guitar Solo 2.2 Skill 1: Wild Chord 2.3 Skill 2: String Bender 2.4 Skill 3: Rock Out! 3 Splash Arts 4 See Also Introduction [ edit ] Originally a talented bard with a burning passion for music, Gao embarked on a new journey to find his purpose in life. When he chanced upon the Battle of Changping, the savage and bloodthirsty monsters shook the bard to his core. From that moment on, ending the war became more important than music. In order to end the war, he formed an anti-Yong alliance. By chance, he also saved Arke, an assassin who had been injured and knocked unconscious. While he heals her wounds with music, she holds nothing back in serving his cause with her sword.
