Following in the footsteps of her elder brother, Sun Ce , Lady Sun grew up with the desire to make life better for all of Jiang. One day, after fighting back against a sudden pirate attack, word of her prowess with the cannon spread far and wide. Be it helping her brother reform the country or repelling enemies, this bold lady is always willing to go all out. Skill 2: Frag Grenade [ edit ] Frag Grenade Skill 2 Ability Type Enfeeble Slow Damage Physical Throws a grenade at the target direction damaging and marking enemies hit, reducing their Physical Defense by 10%.
