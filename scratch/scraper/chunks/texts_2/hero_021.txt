With his expert medical skills, he managed to save countless patients. However, he was later framed by his master and accused of poisoning the Lord of Yong, a betrayal that almost caused his death. Since then, he no longer trusts anyone and instead spends all his time on gruesome drug trials and research into poisons in the name of revenge. Skills [ edit ] Passive Skill: Malicious Practice [ edit ] Malicious Practice Passive Skill Ability Type Mark Heal Damage Magic Skill and Basic Attack hits place up to 5 marks on enemies, each stack dealing damage over time. His Skill 2 and Ultimate place up to 5 marks on himself and his teammates, each stack continually restoring Health over time. Skill 1: Deadly Panacea [ edit ] Deadly Panacea Skill 1 Ability Type Slow Damage Magic Throws a vial of deadly poison at the target location, dealing damage over time and slowing enemies within range. Can store up to 3 vials of poison. Skill 2: Fatal Diagnosis [ edit ] Fatal Diagnosis Skill 2 Ability Type Heal Damage Magic Throws a concoction in the target direction, dealing damage and marking enemies in its path. Heals himself and teammates in its path, while also applying marks . Skill 3: Master of Life [ edit ] Master of Life Skill 3 Ability Type Heal Damage Magic Damages enemies within range and heals teammates. Every mark detonated will increase damage and healing . He recovers extra Health.
