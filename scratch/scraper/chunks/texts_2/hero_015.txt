This man, driven to explore the limits of his sorcerous abilities, later became known as "Master Zhuangzi" and one of the co-founders of the Jixia Academy. Skills [ edit ] Passive Skill: Nature's Will [ edit ] Nature's Will Passive Skill Ability Type Purify Damage Reduction Speed Up Periodly removes crowd control effects and increases Movement Speed and Damage Reduction . Skill 1: Kaleidoscope [ edit ] Kaleidoscope Skill 1 Ability Type Enhance Slow Damage Magic Triggers Nature's Will, releasing dream butterflies in a cone-shaped area, inflicting damage and slowing enemies in range. Skill 2: Sleep Starts [ edit ] Sleep Starts Skill 2 Ability Type Speed Up Damage Magic Damages enemies within range and increase teammate's Movement Speed . Gains a stack each time damage is dealt, up to 5 stacks. Each stack increases skill damage. Passive : Periodically uses Sleep Starts. Skill 3: Butterfly Effect [ edit ] Butterfly Effect Skill 3 Ability Type Cleanse Immunity Sheild Removes crowd control effects from teammates within range and provides a sheild .
